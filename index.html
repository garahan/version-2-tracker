🍎Apple Health sync

How it works

An Apple Shortcut reads your HealthKit data and opens this app with the data as URL parameters. The app auto-fills your metrics and generates smart suggestions based on your real body data.

Setup (5 minutes, one time)

  1. Open the Shortcuts app on iPhone
  2. Create a new Shortcut named BegaHealthSync
  3. Add a Get Health Sample action for each: Steps, Sleep, HRV, Weight, Resting HR, Active Energy, Mindful Minutes, Water
  4. Add a Get Workouts action and set it to total minutes today
  5. Add an Open URL action with the URL pattern shown below, substituting your data variables
  6. Save and add the Shortcut to your home screen for daily one-tap sync
